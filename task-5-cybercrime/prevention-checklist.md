How to Not Get Scammed 

-The Golden Rule: You only enter your UPI PIN to pay, never to receive money.

-Check the Name: When you scan a QR code, look at the name that pops up on your screen before hitting pay.

-App Safety: Only download apps from the official Play Store or App Store.

-Biometrics: Use your fingerprint or face ID to lock your banking apps.

-Stranger Danger: Ignore "urgent" links in SMS or WhatsApp from numbers you don’t know.

-Public Wi-Fi: Don’t check your bank balance or shop while on free public Wi-Fi.

-Lock Your Socials: Use strong, different passwords for every account.

-Daily Limits: Set a small daily spending limit on your UPI app so even if things go wrong, the loss is small.
