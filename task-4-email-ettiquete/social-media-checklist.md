# Social Media Do's and Dont's: 

## DO's:

-Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all accounts.

-Fact-check news articles before sharing them.

-Use LinkedIn to connect with industry professionals.

-Keep personal profile settings strictly private.

-Share academic achievements and project links.

## DON'Ts: 

-Never share passwords, OTPs, or financial details.

-Do not post offensive or discriminatory content.

-Avoid engaging with or promoting cyberbullying.

-Do not tag your live location in real-time.

-Never copy someone else's work without credit.
